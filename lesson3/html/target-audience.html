Target Audience

The target audience for this website is from young adults to more established adults that have an entrepreneurial spirit and a passion for innovation and growth. They may have only gotten a bachelor's and jumped right into owning a business or have owned and ran businesses for a long time. They may be new to the area and looking to find a group of individuals like them or maybe just a place where they would like to network with those similar to them.

Persona 1

The Young Entrepreneur

Age

This younger demographic will range from 22-34 because they more than likely will at the very least have an associate's degree to give them a foundation before entrepreneurship.

Job Title

Founder, CEO, or President of a company

Demographics

Young person, male or female of any race, that has a background of family or friends with a lofty amount of money that are willing to invest. Finished at the very least an associate's degree and a history or entrepreneurship.

Education

At the least an associate's degree but more than likely a backelor's degree.

Experience with the Topic

Wanting a hub where they can meet with other young entrepreneurs.

Family Status

These individuals will either be single, newly married, or married with a young child.

Where, When, and How they use the website

They will more than likely use this website primarily on their smart phones or tablets.

Scenarios

Scenario 1: A young entrepreneur that has a somewhat established business and is wanting to find a place where they can innovate and grow.

Scenario 2: A young entrepreur is moving to a new area and is looking for a place to network with new entrepreneurs.

Scenario 3: A young entrepreneur is wanting to discuss with other professionals about a new business idea and is looking for investors.

Scenario 4: A young entrepreneur feels like they're in a rut and is wanting to learn what other entrepreneurs are doing to stay creative.

Scenario 5: A young entrepreneur wants to become politically active and is looking for a group with like-minded individuals to meet politicians.

Persona 2

The Established Business Owner

Age

This more established demographic ranges from 35 to 60. It is a larger demographic because if these business owners started young they could still be in their mid-30s while some individuals are going to be longer established and could range up to their 60s.

Job Title

Founder or CEO of a long established company

Demographics

Mid-30s to older person, male or female of any race, that has has worked incredibly hard to grow a buesiness and enjoys the company of other business owners.

Education

Could have only received an associate's degree but more than likely has a bachelor's degree or higher in education.

Experience with the Topic

This demographic likes to be present in their close-knit community of business owners and CEO. They help to navigate younger entrepreneurs and find other opportunities of growth for their own business.

Family Status

These individuals are established in their communities. They own a home, have a family, and possibly even grand-children.

Where, When, and How they use the website

They are probably on their laptops more often and view the website on a desktop viewport but may also check events and such on their phone or tablet.

Scenarios

Scenario 1: An established entrepreneur is looking for a place to find younger entrepreneurs to give advice to.

Scenario 2: An older entrepreneur wanting to star present in his community and locate other entrepreneurs.

Scenario 3: A middle-aged entrepreneur looking for a place where they can grow and learn from more established entrepreneurs.

Scenario 4: Older individuals wanting to help their community of business owners start to take part in politics.

Scenario 5: An established entrepreneur opening a new business in the area and wants to network with other established entrepreneurs.
