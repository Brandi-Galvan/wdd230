Color Scheme

View the color scheme on my Coolors Page

Header Color

The background color for the header is Laurel Green and the font is in black.

Nav Color

The background color for the navigation bar is a Dutch White and the font is black. When it is hovered on it takes on the header Laurel Green background color.

Site Background Color

The site background is a really light ivory color called Alabaster that matches the tones of the green and dark white.

Heading Colors

All the h2, h3, and h4 heading have a deep bluish grey color in the Charcoal spectrum. It's hard to distinguish from the black but gives the headings a bit of texture from the rest of the text.
